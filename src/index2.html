dancemusic.wtf

Dancemusic.wtf is a web based quiz game created for us to learn what the different electronic dance music styles sound like. The hopes is that it will help us get a better vocabulary next time we're trying to describe that great track we heard the other night.

Quick Preview

Quick Preview is a small Chrome Extension which enables you to quickly preview a sound before playing it on SoundCloud. Just hover the play button with your mouse and listen to the four samples that will follow.
